Over time, your designers may change their minds about how a search is initiated:

  • Clicking a Search button.
  • Pressing the Enter key
  • After typing a certain number of characters

Since there are many ways of initiating the search, the page should fire its own logical "search" event whenever the search is initiated — no matter how it was done.

By doing this, the controller no longer cares how the search was initiated by the user.
