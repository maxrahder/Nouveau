You may also specify a "belongsTo" association. This creates a getter for the parent record. 

belongsTo : 'Associations.model.Customer'

or

associations : [{
    type : 'belongsTo',
    model : 'Associations.model.Customer'
}]

Then in your code, you may write order.getCustomer() to get a reference to the parent customer record.

There is also a hasOne association.
