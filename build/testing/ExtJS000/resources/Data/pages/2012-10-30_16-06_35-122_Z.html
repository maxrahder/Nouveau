add(date, interval, value):Date

Provides a convenient method for performing basic date arithmetic. 

var released = Ext.Date.parse('1967/06/01', 'Y/m/d');
var taughtTheBandToPlay = Ext.Date.add(released, Ext.Date.YEAR, -20);
var s = Ext.Date.format(taughtTheBandToPlay, 'F j, Y');
alert('Sgt. Pepper taught the band to play on ' + s);

var theNightBefore = Ext.Date.add(released, Ext.Date.DAY, -1);
// Dates increment or decrement appropriately
alert('The night before that was  ' + Ext.Date.format(theNightBefore, 'F j, Y'));
