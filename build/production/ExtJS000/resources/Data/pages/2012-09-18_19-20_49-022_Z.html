Choosing the best class to extend is a matter of choosing the simplest component class that meets your needs.

  • Extend Ext.container.Container when you need a simple container
  • Ext.panel.Panel has additional capabilities:
    • title
    • docked items (tbar, rbar, bbar, lbar, fbar)
    • buttons[]
    • tools[]
    • expand/collapse

Typically, when you subclass a component you initialize properties or override methods, as needed.
