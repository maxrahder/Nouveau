An indefinite progress bar indicator

Ext.Msg.wait() can only be closed programmatically.

var m = Ext.Msg.wait("Please wait while we process your application...", "Processing", {
   interval: 300, //bar will move fast!
   duration: 3000,
   increment: 10,
   text: 'Updating...',
 
   fn: function(){
      m.hide();
   }
});
