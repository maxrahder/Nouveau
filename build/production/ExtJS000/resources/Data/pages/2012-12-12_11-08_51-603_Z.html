Introduction

With a Sencha profile you can access the Sencha forums, Sencha IO, Marketplace and Developer directory.

Steps

In case you don't have a Sencha profile yet, you can create one for free with the steps below. Otherwise you can skip the first step.

Create a Sencha profile

  1. Register

    Navigate with your browser to: http://www.sencha.com/forum/register.php

  2. Fill in the form and accept the forum rules

Login

  1. Go to http://www.sencha.com/forum
  2. Login with your Sencha account
  3. Go to settings > Networking > Friend & Contacts

    Add your instructors username to your friendlist

  4. Go to settings > My Profile > Edit profile

    Edit your profile.

API Docs

  1. Navigate to: http://docs.sencha.com/ext-js/
  2. Login with your Sencha user account
  3. In the Docs, search for proxy

    Scroll down and notice you that it's possible to reply on topics

Market

  1. Navigate to: https://market.sencha.com/
  2. Login with your Sencha user account
  3. Browse through Components
  4. Click Product and filter for Ext JS 4.1

    Notice the amount of extensions you can download and use
