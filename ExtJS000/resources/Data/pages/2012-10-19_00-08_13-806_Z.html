tpl allows you to specify a "tokenized" HTML snippet as the content of the element. Under program control you can replace the tokens with values.

We'll talk in depth about templates later in the week.

var c = Ext.create('Ext.Component', {

    //data: {name: 'Joe'}, 
    tpl: 'Hi <b>{name}</b>',

    renderTo : Ext.getBody()

});
c.update({name: 'Fred'});
