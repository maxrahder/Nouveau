You can also listen to low-level DOM events.

For example, open the debugger console and copy-and-paste, and run this code:

Ext.select('p').on('click', function(event, target){
  Ext.fly(target).fadeOut();
});

This code selects all paragraphs, and for each, listens to the click event. If you click on a paragraph, the handler runs the Ext.dom.Element#highlight() method on the clicked paragraph.
