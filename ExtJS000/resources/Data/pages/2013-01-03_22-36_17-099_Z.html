• Sencha MVC lets you separate your code into models, views and controllers
    • Ext.data.Store definitions are placed in the "store" folder
    • Ext.data.Model definitions are placed in the "model" folder
    • Ext.app.Controller definitions are placed in the "controller" folder
  • Encapsulate major components into their own classes
  • Think in terms of the API you want the calling routine to use
  • It's possible to fire custom events from the view, the controller can listen to these.
