• Illuminations for Developers was created by Steven Roussey, one of the volunteer developers of Firebug. Illuminations currently only works in FireFox.
  • It is VERY useful for inspecting Ext JS components and other Ext JS objects.
  • In this example, we are able to look at the components inside a YelpExtplorer.view.Viewport, and at the properties of the YelpExtplorer.store.OnlineCategories store (highlighted)
  • Documentation and installation: http://www.illuminations-for-developers.com/
