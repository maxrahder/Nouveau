• Ext JS 4 uses Sass and Compass to generate CSS files
  • Themes are located in the packages folder
  • All themes extend from the base theme.
  • Classic and Neptune theme extend from the neutral theme
  • You can quickly apply a custom color scheme to your application by setting the
    $base-color variable
  • Sencha Cmd can compile your Sass stylesheet: 
    sencha ant sass
  • Sencha Cmd optimizes your stylesheet for production, while building the app: 
    sencha app build
