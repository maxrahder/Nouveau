• Sencha.io Login

    Users are at the heart of all applications, and Login makes it easy to provide accounts for your users and to store data with their account, as well as register new users from within your app. Login takes the hassle out of social login as well, with Facebook and Twitter login coming soon.

  • Sencha.io Data

    Store and share data between devices and between users. Using a new proxy that comes in the Sencha.io SDK, your applications data is synchronized and sharable in the cloud all through your existing models.

  • Sencha.io Messages

    Send messages to other users as simple JSON objects, and simply register a call back to receive inbound messages. If your application is offline, messages will queue on device or in the cloud waiting for delivery.

  • Sencha.io Deployment

    Need a place to run your application? Sencha.io provides a platform for you to deploy your Sencha Touch application, as well as a test environment for you to collaborate with you team while building it.
