Timesavers with Sencha Cmd

The following are not all that you can do with Sencha Cmd but they give you an idea of what you can do to save some time and stub out your project.

We are essentially going to revisit the YelpExtplorer project and set everything up to build out the project in a few command lines of code. We will use two features of Sencha Cmd to do this.

We will discuss them in brief first and then show you what to do.
